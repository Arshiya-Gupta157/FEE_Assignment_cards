Notebook

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia This article is about the writing pad. For the computer, often called a notebook, see Laptop. For other uses, see Notebook (disambiguation). "Notepad" redirects here. For the Microsoft program, see Windows Notepad. For other uses, see Notepad (disambiguation). A notebook (also known as a notepad, writing pad, drawing pad, or legal pad) is a book or stack of paper pages that are often ruled and used for purposes such as note-taking, journaling or other writing, drawing, or scrapbooking..
